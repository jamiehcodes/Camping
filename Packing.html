Suggested Packing List

Sleeping/General:

  • Tent
  • Sleeping bags
  • Sleeping pads or cots (I like using a pad on top of the cot for extra warmth and comfort)
  • Pillows
  • Head Lamp/Lantern/Flashlight & extra batteries (for when you need to use the restroom at night!)
  • Hammock (Very relaxing to stare up at the stars)
  • Foldable chairs

Optional:

  • Extra mat under the tent
  • Extra blankets
  • Inner tube, goggles, and other water play items, if going in water

Personal:

  • Hand sanitizer
  • Water Wipes or Baby Wipes
  • Toothpaste and Toothbrush
  • Sunscreen
  • Bug repellent
  • Contact Lenses, Case, Solution, Glasses (if needed)
  • Toilet Paper (just in case the bathroom is out)
  • Towels
  • Bar Soap
  • Hairbrush
  • If baby is coming: Diaper, wipes, bottles, formula, foldable changing pad

Clothing:

  • Jackets
  • Hats and/or Visors for Hiking
  • Socks, Underwear, Shirts, Pants, Shorts
  • Hiking Shoes and Sandals/Water Shoes
  • Bathing Suit (if going in water)
  • Snow Bib for kids (it can get cold at night!)
  • Hat/Beanies, Mittens/Gloves, Scarves
  • Thermals

Meal Times:

  • Gallons of water with refillable drink containers
  • Ice chest (can buy more ice at stores, if needed)
  • Utensils, cooking utensils, napkins, plates, bowls, cups
  • Table ClothTable Cloth
  • Ziploc Bags (or environmentally friendly alternative)
  • Trash Bags
  • Biodegradable Dish Soap, if not using disposable items
  • Firewood (unless you can find enough nearby)
  • Lighter and Lighter Fluid
  • Portable Stove and Pots/Pans to cook
  • Butane or Propane (depending on your stove)
  • Cooking Oil and Condiments (salt & pepper)
  • Aluminum Foil, Iron Skllet with METAL spatula, and/or Skewers if cooking over fire

Food Ideas:

  • Burgers (meat, lettuce, tomato, buns, cheese)
  • Sandwiches (e.g. PBJ, Avocado & Tomato, Deli Meat + cheese)
  • Porridge
  • Eggs with Breakfast Potatoes
  • Macaroni & Cheese
  • Ramen
  • Oatmeal
  • Pancakes

Snack Ideas:

  • Chips
  • Granola/Protein Bars
  • Popcorn
  • Fruits (Apples, Bananas, Cuties
